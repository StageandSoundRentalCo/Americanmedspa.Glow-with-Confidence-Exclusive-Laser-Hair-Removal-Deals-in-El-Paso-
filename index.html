The Science Behind Laser Hair Removal

You may be curious about how exactly laser hair removal works. Here’s a quick breakdown of the process:

  • Laser Technology: The laser emits light that’s absorbed by the pigment (melanin) in the hair. The light is converted into heat, which damages the hair follicle and inhibits future growth.
  • Hair Growth Phases: Hair grows in cycles, and laser hair removal is most effective during the active growth phase. This is why multiple sessions are necessary to target all the hair follicles and achieve permanent results.
  • Safety and Precision: Modern laser technology is designed to be safe for all skin types. During the procedure, your skin is protected by a cooling device, and our trained specialists ensure the treatment is performed with the highest level of care.
